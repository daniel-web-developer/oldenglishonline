About This Site

Old English is the earliest attested form of the English language, and is very different from Modern English, making it difficult even for native English speakers to understand. This website is designed for those looking to learn Old English, whether they are complete beginners or more advanced learners. It will introduce you, topic by topic, to the structure and sound of the Old English language in easy to digest chunks with plenty of oppertunity to practice along the way.

Start from the Beginning

If you are new to Old English, or want to start with the basics, you should start here!
