About

Learning Old English Online was designed and developed by Victoria Koivisto-Kokko at University College Cork as part of her Master’s thesis. The aim of her research was first to ascertain the best design for an online learning course aimed at teaching medieval languages, then to see how far the design could be made extensible by keeping content seperate from the framework used to display it. Using Old English as the core subject, a course design and environmental design was researched, built, and then tested, with the end result being this website.

Currently, only the module ‘Cases and Strong Masculine Nouns’ is populated. However, after the completion of the MA, the project will continue to be expanded both in terms of the underlying technical framework and the content.

For those interested in additional learning resources, you can find a comprehensive overview of Old English resources on the website of the Teachers of Old English in Britain and Ireland: http://www.toebi.org.uk/resources/
