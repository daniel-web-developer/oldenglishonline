Cambridge R9.17. - Dicts of Cato

  • Title: Dicts of Cato
  • Folios: f.045r - f.045v
  • Size (cm): 22 x 16
  • Languages: Latin and Old English
  • Attribution: Trinity College, Cambridge
  • Shelfmark: R.9.17

Scroll or pinch to zoom

Ne flit ðu

wið anwilne monn ne wið ofer spræcne manegum menn

is forgifen ðæt he spræcan mæg ond swiðe feawum ðæt he

seo gescead wis. Wite ðæs maran ðanc ðe ðu hæbbe ðonne

ðæs þe ðe monn gehate, ne hopa þu to swiðe to ðam þe

mon gehate. Ðær lytel gehaten bið þær bið lytel alogen.
