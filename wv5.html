Weak Verbs III

Class III consists of four verbs: habban - to have, libban - to live, secgan - to say, and hycgan - to think. These verbs do not conjugate in the same way as each other, and can combine features of the first two weak classes, so they need to be learned individually.

  • Lufian
  • Andswarian
  • Timbrian
  • Ricsian
